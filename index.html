About Us

Pearls has long stood as the crown jewel of the Los Santos coastline. Nestled along the iconic Del Perro Pier, our mission is simple: to offer a refined escape where world-class cuisine meets oceanfront elegance.

From sustainably sourced seafood to the craftsmanship of our elite culinary team, every dish is prepared with precision and passion. Whether you're savoring our Atlantic-caught cod or indulging in our signature Key Lime Pie, each bite is designed to create a lasting memory.

With ambient lighting and outdoor seating on our scenic terrace, Pearls is the ultimate destination for romantic evenings, private gatherings, and unforgettable celebrations by the sea.
